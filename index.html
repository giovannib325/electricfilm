Detailed Services

Smart (Switchable) Film

PDLC film that turns from clear to opaque with a switch — ideal for conference rooms, bathrooms, and showrooms. Integrates with wall switches, remotes, or home automation systems.

Solar Control Films

Spectrally selective films reduce heat while preserving natural light. Great for El Paso’s climate to lower HVAC costs.

Safety & Security Films

Thicker adhesive films that hold glass together on impact — helps protect occupants and deter break-ins.

Decorative Films

Custom patterns and frosts for branding, signage, or interior design accents.
